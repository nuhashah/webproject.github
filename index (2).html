Meet Our Instructors

Sensei Akira

With over 30 years of experience in karate, Sensei Akira brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our dojo. He specializes in Shotokan Karate and emphasizes the importance of mental discipline along with physical skill.

Sensei Hina

Sensei Hina is known for her dynamic teaching style and her focus on self-defense techniques. She encourages students to push their limits and believes in the transformative power of martial arts.

Our Classes

Our dojo offers a range of classes for all ages and skill levels. From beginners to advanced practitioners, we have something for everyone. Our classes include:

  • Kids Karate: Focusing on fun, discipline, and basic skills.
  • Adult Karate: Comprehensive training in techniques and forms.
  • Self-Defense: Practical skills for personal safety.
  • Advanced Competition Training: For those looking to compete at a higher level.
